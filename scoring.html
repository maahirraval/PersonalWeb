History of Scoring

The three-point line has revolutionized modern basketball. More and more teams in modern NBA teams are using the three-point line more frequently as a strategy. Players like Stephen Curry and Klay Thompson have shown how deadly the three-point line can be. These two players effectively helped the Golden State Warriors create a dynasty. Winning four championships and going to the finals five times. Stephen Curry, in particular, had a huge influence on the next generation as the use of three-pointers has exponentially grown over the last several years especially.
